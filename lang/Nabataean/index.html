Nabataean Alphabet

What's Nabataean?

The Nabataean alphabet is an alphabet used for some dialects of Aramaic. It is primarily known for being the system which modern Arabic writing is ultimately derived from. The Arabic alphabet is a derivative of a cursive form of Nabataean.
