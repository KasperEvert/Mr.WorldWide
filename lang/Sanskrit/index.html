Transliteration

Let's take a example word. So for example महाभारत. Now this word has plenty of "ा" which either makes a "h" or more commonly an "a" sound. It's a character that you append onto a character, like for example n + ^ = nâ. So the sentence then becomes (when split up): म=M, हा=Ha, भा=Ba, र =R, त=T. This word would transliterate into mahabharata, which is the title of a famous book written with Devanagari.
