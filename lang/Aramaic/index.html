Aramaic Alphabet
(𐡀𐡋𐡐 𐡁𐡉𐡕 𐡀𐡓𐡌𐡉𐡉𐡀 - Alef beith Arameyaya)

Does Aramaic have an alphabet?

No. Thus there are a couple of related semitic alphabets down below who all have similar structure. One of which is the Imperial Aramaic alphabet which is probably what people mean if they ever say the "aramaic" alphabet. Aramaic also often use one of the three main Syriac alphabets, which have their own page.

What does "Alef beith" mean?

So "Alef beith" is just the name of the first two letters of the alphabet. Just like our word in english "Alphabet" is just the name of the first to letters of the Greek alphabet (which the roman one is based upon): Alpha Beta.
