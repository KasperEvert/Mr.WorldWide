Is it a difficult script to learn?

The hebrew alphabet is kinda difficult to learn, but the yiddish one is quite a bit simpler. The letter Ayin for example, is in hebrew quite difficult since it doesn't really have a single sound, the letter Ayin essentially makes any vowel sound in hebrew, but in yiddish it's almost always an E sound. Really the yiddish alphabet is no harder to learn than the greek or roman or russian alphabet(s).
