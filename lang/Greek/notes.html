Οι σημειώσεις, The notes

Masculine DA: ο Ending: -σ, -ει?
Feminine DA: η Ending: -η, -α*, -ου
Neuter DA: το Ending: -ο, -ι, -μα*
* Feminine -α and Neuter -μα can be confused sometimes
We Ending: -με
I Ending: -ω
They Ending: -ουν
You Ending: -ις, -τε?
