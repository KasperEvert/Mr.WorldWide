This page is a page about the meanings of the Hebrew letters along with how to spell each letter in Hebrew. Some like ב are both what the pictogram is supposed to represent, and what it means in modern Hebrew. Some are either just modern meaning or what the pictogram depicts. Sorry for mixing those.
