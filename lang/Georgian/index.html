Georgian Alphabet
(Ⴕართული Ⴀნბანი - Kartuli Anbani)

Is it a difficult script to learn?

The Georgian alphabet is has some small relation to the Greek one, which means that a few letters might be easy to learn. For the most part it is a medium difficulty script.

Practice 1k most common words

What does "Anbani" mean?

So "Anbani" is just the name of the first two letters of the alphabet. Just like our word in english "Alphabet" is just the name of the first to letters of the Greek alphabet (which the roman one is based upon): Alpha Beta.
