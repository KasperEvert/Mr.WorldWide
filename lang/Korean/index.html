Korean Alphabet
(한글 - Hangeul)
(조선글 - Joseongeul)

Is it a difficult script to learn?

The Korean writing system, in the south called: Hangeul, in the north: Joseongeul, is usually considered one of the easiest writing systems to learn for learners of all language backgrounds. If you don't speak any east asian language some of these sounds might be unfamiliar to you and you might have trouble making the correct sounds. But it's still considered to be a very easy script to learn, partly due to the fact it divides vowles and consonants into easily distinguishable groups.
